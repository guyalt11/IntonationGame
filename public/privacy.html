Privacy Policy – EarTune

EarTune (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This app is designed for ear training and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information Storage

  • All game data, high scores, and preferences (sound, difficulty, scale, etc.) are stored locally on your device using secure local storage.
  • None of this data is transmitted to any server or shared with third parties.

Children

EarTune is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page.
